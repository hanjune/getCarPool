Why car pool?

ENVIRONMENTAL FRIENDLY

Car pooling will reduce number of car on the roads and hence reduce carbon emission that causes global warming.

SAVE TIME

Fewer cars on the road will improve traffic condition. This will get you to the destination in shorter period of time.

SAVE MONEY

Sharing is caring. Sharing petrol fees makes everyone life easier.

MEET NEW FRIENDS

Hop on the car you found on CarPool and say hi to the driver. Chit chat on your ride and you might meet someone interesting that you want to keep in touch with.

SAFETY

We verify every online user with university email accounts so that they are students just like you. Drivers are also require to submit their driving license in order to make offer. We strive to make sure users safety is protected and eliminate creepy people without identification.
